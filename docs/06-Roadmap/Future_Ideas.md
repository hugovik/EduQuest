# Future Ideas

---

# Purpose

This document captures ideas, concepts, and opportunities that may shape the future evolution of EduQuest.

Unlike the Product Roadmap, these ideas are intentionally exploratory and are not committed to a specific release or milestone.

The goal is to preserve creativity while maintaining a clear distinction between planned work and future possibilities.

---

# Vision

EduQuest should remain an evolving educational platform.

As technology advances and educational needs change, the platform should continuously explore new ways to make learning more engaging, accessible, and personalized.

Every idea should ultimately support one objective:

**Help children enjoy learning.**

---

# Guiding Principles

Future ideas should:

- Improve learning outcomes
- Encourage curiosity
- Support creativity
- Remain child-friendly
- Preserve simplicity
- Integrate naturally
- Avoid unnecessary complexity
- Align with the EduQuest vision

# Idea Evaluation

Future ideas should be evaluated using the following questions:

- Does it improve learning?
- Is it enjoyable?
- Is it accessible?
- Does it fit the EduQuest world?
- Is it technically sustainable?
- Will children benefit from it?

Ideas that do not support the educational mission should not be pursued.
